Privacy Policy

Last updated July 16, 2026.

Lima 3D does not ask visitors to create accounts and does not operate an application analytics or advertising system. The hosting server may retain ordinary security and access logs such as request time, requested path, IP address, user agent, and response status.

Google Maps Platform

When Google Reality or Street View is activated, the browser connects directly to Google Maps Platform to retrieve 3D, map, and panorama content. Google may process technical and usage information under the Google Privacy Policy. Google’s attribution and data-provider credits appear inside the relevant viewer.

Other map sources

The open-data mode requests map or imagery resources from the providers named in its on-screen attribution. Those providers receive the network information normally required to answer a browser request.

Local browser state

The application uses transient in-memory state for the camera, selected layers, performance adaptation, and viewer controls. It does not intentionally store Street View imagery or Google 3D content.

Questions

Questions may be directed through the project repository linked from the application documentation.
